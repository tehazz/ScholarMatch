About This System

What does ScholarMatch do?

ScholarMatch helps students receive a suggested scholarship option based on information they provide. The current recommendation model focuses on three main options: MARA, PTPTN, and JPA.


Additional scholarship opportunities for Malaysian and international students are listed on the IIUM Scholarships page as references.

How It Works

Simple Process

  • Student enters profile information.
  • The system processes the submitted details.
  • The trained recommendation model suggests a scholarship.
  • The student can view more scholarship details and references.
