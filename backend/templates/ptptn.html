PTPTN Loan

PTPTN provides education financing assistance to Malaysian students pursuing higher education.

Announcement

PTPTN education financing is commonly used by Malaysian university students to support tuition fees and study-related expenses. Students are advised to check the official PTPTN website for updated application procedures, repayment information, and eligibility terms.

General Eligibility

  • Malaysian citizen.
  • Registered as a student in an eligible higher education institution.
  • Must meet PTPTN application requirements.
  • Household income may influence financing amount or eligibility category.
  • Must submit complete application and supporting documents.

Required Documents

  • Copy of identification card.
  • University offer letter or study confirmation letter.
  • Bank account details.
  • Household income information, if required.
  • Other supporting documents requested by PTPTN.
