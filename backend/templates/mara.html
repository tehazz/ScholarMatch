Scholarship / Education Assistance

MARA

MARA provides education sponsorship and financial assistance opportunities for eligible Malaysian students.

Announcement

MARA education sponsorship is commonly considered by students who require financial support and meet the required academic and eligibility conditions. Students are advised to refer to the official MARA website for the most updated intake announcements and application dates.

General Eligibility

  • Malaysian citizen.
  • Good academic performance.
  • Priority may be given to students from eligible income backgrounds.
  • Must meet specific programme or field requirements set by MARA.
  • Must submit complete supporting documents during application.

Required Documents

  • Copy of identification card.
  • Academic transcript or latest examination result.
  • University offer letter or confirmation letter.
  • Household income proof or salary statement.
  • Co-curricular or achievement certificates, if applicable.

Scholarship Summary

Focus: Academic performance and financial background

Suitable For: Students who meet MARA eligibility requirements
